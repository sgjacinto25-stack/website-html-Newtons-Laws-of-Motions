Welcome to Newton's Physics Website
Isaac Newton's three laws of motion, published in 1687, are the foundation of classical mechanics. They describe the relationship between a body, the forces acting on it, and its motion. The laws explain inertia, the relationship between force and acceleration (F = ma), and action–reaction pairs.

Isaac Newton

First Law

Law of Inertia
